Welcome to RelDeep !

Goal

• Our goal is to make human relationships stronger & deeper by recommending clever & specific personlized daily actions.
• We act like a mentor/life coach to you. Who serve you with daily actions to take, to make you more deeply connected withy our own people.
• Also like a mentor/life coach we want to serve people by modifying the way they look at things in +ve way.


Some FAQs.

Why we need to keep connected with people?

• A real connection with live thing is required. Whether it's cat, dog or a human. But human have kind of same problems & emotions as you have. So, that should be 1st choice.
• A deep connection is a win-win. Both person get chance to share their happiness & problems.
• Now here's a cool thing. When you share problems, total stress is divided between people. Unlike, when you share happiness, joy remains same as everyone feel same amount of joy.
• Joy only increases as we share, while tension is divided as we share.


Why do healthy relationships matter?

• Relationship is the highest correlated factor with your happiness & mental health. Not fame, huge bungalow etc.
• No matter how much one have achieved in terms of money, fame, power; if he/she isn't deeply connected with some people & can't share his/her problems, happiness with others, a true satisfaction can't be achieved.
• So, ideally, healthy relationships with some people should be the very first priority of everyone.


How we recommend actions?

• In simple terms, we take all the data provided by you, we analyse it, and we give (naive + smart) recommendaions keeping in mind, ideally, what should be the case.
• Like, ideally, you should be more close to your parents or partner or child. If you're not, we tell you to take these actions to connect more with them.
• Also, we look at other people's reactions to your actions; to predict, whether you two should even connect or not in the first place.
• Ultimately, just like a mentor/life coach do, our goal is to modify people's mindset in +ve way by giving new perspectives to look at things.
